About

Building at the intersection of business and technology.

I am a final-year BCom Information Systems student at the University of Johannesburg with a cumulative average of 83.2%. Alongside my studies, I founded ArcaneEdge, an innovative marketing and tech solutions company that specialises in combining brand strategy, digital marketing, and AI-driven technology.

Our flagship innovation, the Digital Doppelgänger, simulates consumer behaviour before launch, optimising campaigns to increase engagement, reduce wasted spend by up to 30%, and grow revenue sustainably. The platform holds a provisional patent and is targeting retail, FMCG, and financial services sectors.

I won 1st place nationally at the Mr Price Bindzu Youth Fund competition (R1,000,000) and 1st in Gauteng (R90,000), validating both the product and the business model.

VitalEdge Fitness Database

A full relational database system designed for a South African fitness and wellness company built around a sponsor-funded membership model. The database handles member enrolment across multiple branches, group fitness classes, personal training, and automated stock replenishment for supplement and gear shops.
