About

Building at the intersection of business and technology.

I am a final-year BCom Information Systems student at the University of Johannesburg with a cumulative average of 83.2%. I am also the Founder and CEO of ArcaneEdge, an innovative marketing and tech solutions company. We specialise in combining brand strategy, digital marketing, and AI-driven technology.

Our flagship innovation, the Digital Doppelgänger, simulates consumer behaviour before launch, optimising campaigns to increase engagement, reduce wasted spend by up to 30%, and grow revenue sustainably. The platform holds a provisional patent and is targeting retail, FMCG, and financial services sectors.

I won 1st place nationally at the Mr Price Bindzu Youth Fund competition (R1,000,000) and 1st in Gauteng (R90,000), validating both the product and the business model.

Non-Profit · Founder

Becoming Her is a women's empowerment non-profit dedicated to equipping young women with the confidence, skills, and strategic mindset to thrive personally and professionally.

We believe empowerment must be holistic — financially, physically, mentally, and spiritually. Our mission is to create safe, structured spaces where young women are mentored, upskilled, and supported in becoming leaders within their communities and industries.

Through mentorship programmes, skills workshops, entrepreneurship exposure, and personal growth sessions, we address mindset, financial literacy, digital skills, and leadership — because empowerment is not just inspiration. It is access, guidance, and opportunity.

Our long-term vision is a generation of confident, economically active, and socially impactful women who lead with purpose and resilience. They're the women they've always been — empowered and thriving, holistically.
